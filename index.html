Tribute to Pa Ajani Bashir Ogunshola

By Ogunshola Taiwo

MY LOVELY FATHER

Pa Ogunshola Ajani Bashir was a wonderful man of timbre and caliber. A dotting father who irrespective of circumstances, widespread thing always made sure people around him were well taken care of. Sadly death never let us enjoy his amazing personality for long. Keep resting in the bosom of the Lord dearest father. Till we meet to part no more!!!
